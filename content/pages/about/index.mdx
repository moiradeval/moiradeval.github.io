1.Moira is a visual artist and creative director with a Master’s degree from Brunel University London.
Her professional practice centers on photography and brand design, where she leads a studio-based team delivering visual strategy, creative direction, and production for brands and organizations.
Alongside her commercial work, she maintains an independent oil painting practice focused on long-term visual research and personal narrative.
Her background bridges academic training, creative entrepreneurship, and fine art practice, positioning her work at the intersection of art, design, and brand storytelling.

2.A Calgary-based visual artist and brand designer working across branding, photography, graphic design, and oil painting. She began her fine art training at the age of 18 and holds a Master’s degree from Brunel University (UK), where she developed a strong foundation in visual language and conceptual thinking.
She runs a professional photography studio with a dedicated creative team, delivering brand-focused commercial and creative projects. Alongside her client work, she maintains an independent oil painting practice, using painting as a space for deeper, long-term artistic exploration.
Her work balances strategy and emotion, combining technical discipline with expressive freedom. Whether building a brand or creating a painting, she focuses on clarity, connection, and visual impact.
