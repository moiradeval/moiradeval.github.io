Moira is a visual artist and creative director with a Master’s degree from Brunel University London. Her professional practice centers on photography and brand design, where she leads a studio-based team delivering visual strategy, creative direction, and production for brands and organizations. Alongside her commercial work, she maintains an independent oil painting practice focused on long-term visual research and personal narrative. Her background bridges academic training, creative entrepreneurship, and fine art practice, positioning her work at the intersection of art, design, and brand storytelling.
